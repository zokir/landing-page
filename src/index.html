Take Ownership and Control Back of Your Social Data

Lumeos is a decentralized social platform where users own their data, control their privacy, and can monetize its access.

Read the Whitepaper Join Our Telegram

Privacy

You’re in full control of who is accessing your data.

Privacy

By owning their own data, users also are in full control on who can access their info, whether free, or in exchange of tokens. The only users that can access the user’s data has to own a key that the user provide them.Lumeos provides all users an intuitive User Interface (GUI) that allows them to set who has access to any of their data. In addition, this is an opt-in feature, rather than an opt-out feature like in many social networks, to ensure that the user’s data are private from day 1, and only change access when the user requests it.

Data monetization

Earn tokens by allowing users to view some of your data.

Data monetization

Now that the user owns their data, they can sell it to anyone that requests it. They have the option to anonymize it as well, so that it’s only available as part of an aggregated data. (for instance a user can buy the result of a 100-answered poll but they cannot see what each individual user has answered)

Proof of Friendship

They are also incentivized to invite their friends and family members, and both parties will be given a small amount of tokens as well after they sign up. The concept of Proof of Friendship ensures that users with more friends can make more tokens on their data, compared to users with less friends.
